Community Owned

Kind Cornwall will be 100% community owned and controlled by our members to meet our shared needs. We will operate as a co-op and every co-op across the world shares the same values.

Co-ops are owned by the people closest to the business, not distant investors only interested in a financial return. These are typically the employees, customers or local community. This means co-ops focus not just on making a profit, but how they made it and what they do with it to bring value to their members and community.

And because we are a social enterprise all our profits will be re-invested to making Cornwall even better.
